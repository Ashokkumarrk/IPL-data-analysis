Key Insights from IPL Data Analysis( 2008 - 2024)¶

In [ ]:
1. 🏆 Chennai Super Kings and Mumbai Indians are the most consistent teams in IPL history.
2. 🎯 Toss winners have a slight edge — they win the match over 50% of the time.
3. 👑 Virat Kohli and MS Dhoni dominate the Player of the Match awards.
4. 🧠 Each team has a unique match-winner: Rohit (MI), Dhoni (CSK), Samson (RR), etc.
5. 🏟 Wankhede, Eden Gardens, and Chepauk are the most-used IPL venues.
6. 🔥 Super Over matches are becoming more common — especially in 2019 and 2020.
7. ⚖ Umpires like Nitin Menon and S Ravi have officiated the most games.
8. 📈 Some seasons (like 2020) had fewer matches due to external events (e.g., COVID-19).
9. 🎯 Team win trends show CSK, MI dominance, with GT rising in 2022.
10. 🧪 Insights like these help understand the strategy, thrill, and structure of IPL data.
11. Most teams prefer chasing targets - especially when dew is a factor.win percentage is slightly higher while chasing.
